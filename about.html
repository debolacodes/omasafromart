About Oma's Afromart
Your Home for Authentic Nigerian & African Foodstuff!
Oma's Afromart is an online grocery store that brings the rich flavors of Nigeria and Africa straight to your kitchen. Based in Lincoln, Nebraska, we specialize in high-quality African food products, spices, snacks, drinks and essential grocery items that connect you to home. Whether you're craving honey beans, egusi, ofada rice, or traditional snacks like chin chin and kuli kuli, we've got you covered.
Founded with a passion for culture and community, Oma's AfroMart is more than just a grocery store – it's a celebration of heritage. We proudly serve individuals and families looking for fresh, carefully sourced ingredients and delicious flavors rooted in tradition.
Our Goal is to provide a convenient and reliable shopping experience for all your African food needs. We understand the importance of quality and authenticity, which is why we work closely with trusted suppliers to ensure that every product meets our high standards.
We believe everyone deserves a taste of home, and that's why we offer fast delivery, excellent customer service, and a growing selection of African favorites. Whether you're shopping for everyday meals or planning a special occasion, Oma's AfroMart is here to make your cooking experience simple, authentic, and enjoyable.
Shop with us today and taste the difference. Free delivery available within Lincoln, Nebraska!.
Our Mission
At Oma's AfroMart, our mission is to deliver high-quality, authentic African food products to homes across the U.S. while celebrating culture, promoting healthy eating, and providing exceptional customer service with every order
Our Vision
To be the leading provider of authentic African food products in the U.S, connecting communities to the rich flavors of Africa with quality, convenience, and cultural pride.
