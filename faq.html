FAQ

Find answers to common questions about our products, shipping, and services.

What is Oma's Afromart?

Oma's Afromart is an online store for African groceries, pantry staples, and specialty products.

Where are you located?

We are based in the United States and ship nationwide. Some items may have regional delivery options.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes. We offer standard shipping and, where available, local delivery or pickup options.

How can I place an order?

Browse our catalog, add items to your cart, and checkout with your preferred payment method.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major debit/credit cards and other common online payment options at checkout.

How long does shipping take?

Orders typically ship within 1–3 business days. Delivery times depend on your location and the carrier.

Are your products FDA compliant?

We work with trusted suppliers and importers that follow applicable regulations for food safety and labeling.

Can I buy in bulk or wholesale?

Yes. Please contact us with your list and quantities so we can provide a quote and availability.

Do you accept returns or exchanges?

Non-perishable items are returnable within our policy window if unopened and in original condition. Perishables are not returnable.

How can I contact you?

Email: info@omasafromart.com • Phone: +1 (224) 431-3658 • Or use the contact form on our website.
